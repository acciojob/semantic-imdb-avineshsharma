Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is given a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can perform an impossible task — planting an idea into someone's mind, known as "inception".
